Basics

What is OxPoints?

OxPoints is a dataset containing information about organisations and places within the University. It is designed so that the data can be easily incorporated into other systems.

At the moment it contains such things as names, addresses, locations and homepages. These data are available for reuse through a simple API.

For more information, please see our public documentation.

What if I want to add information about something OxPoints doesn't model?

Simply put, get in touch with your proposal and we'll see what we can do.

What are the types?

University
Only used for the University of Oxford. You cannot create entities of this type.
College
A constituent college or society of the University, as defined by Statute V.
PPH
A Permanent Private Hall of the University, as defined by Statute V.
Department, Faculty, Unit
Mostly interchangeable, depending on the name or inclination of the unit in question.
StudentGroup
A registered society or club.
